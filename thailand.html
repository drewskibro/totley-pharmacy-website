HEALTH INFORMATION

Malaria and other health risks in Thailand

Malaria risk

Malaria risk in Thailand is very low in most tourist destinations including Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket, and Koh Samui. Risk is higher in rural forested areas near the borders with Myanmar, Laos, and Cambodia. If you're visiting these areas, we can prescribe antimalarial medication.

Other considerations

  • Dengue fever is present throughout Thailand year-round
  • Japanese encephalitis risk in rural and agricultural areas
  • Avoid untreated water and ice from unknown sources
  • Be cautious with street food hygiene
  • Comprehensive travel insurance strongly recommended
