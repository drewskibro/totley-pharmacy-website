BEFORE YOU VISIT

Quick answers to help you prepare

Everything you need to know before your visit to our Sheffield clinic

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended for weight loss consultations and travel health. Walk-ins welcome for prescriptions and some vaccinations.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have free parking available on-site for all patients visiting our Totley clinic.

What should I bring?

Bring any relevant medical records, current medications, and ID. For travel health, bring your travel itinerary.

Do you accept NHS prescriptions?

Yes, we're an NHS pharmacy and accept all NHS prescriptions. We also offer private prescriptions and services.
