MOUNJARO & WEGOVY IN SHEFFIELD

What are Mounjaro and Wegovy?

Prescription GLP-1 medications proven to help Sheffield patients achieve sustainable weight loss

Mounjaro

Tirzepatide

Mounjaro Sheffield is a dual-action GLP-1 medication (tirzepatide) that targets both GLP-1 and GIP receptors. Clinical trials show patients lose an average of 15-20% of their body weight.

  • Dual-action formula for enhanced results
  • Once-weekly injection
  • Available in Sheffield with prescription

Mounjaro Sheffield: Book your consultation to see if you're suitable

Wegovy

Semaglutide

Wegovy Sheffield is a proven GLP-1 medication (semaglutide) that helps control appetite and blood sugar. Clinical studies show average weight loss of 10-15% of body weight.

  • Clinically proven GLP-1 treatment
  • Once-weekly injection
  • May be available on NHS in Sheffield

Wegovy Sheffield: Direct access to treatment without NHS waiting lists

How Mounjaro & Wegovy Work

Both Mounjaro and Wegovy are GLP-1 receptor agonists that work by:

  • Reducing appetite and food cravings
  • Slowing stomach emptying to help you feel fuller longer
  • Improving blood sugar control
  • Supporting sustainable, long-term weight loss

You may be suitable if you have a BMI of 30 or above, or 27+ with weight-related health conditions such as high blood pressure or type 2 diabetes. We'll assess your eligibility during your free consultation.

Consultation fees and medication costs vary depending on your treatment plan. We'll provide full pricing during your initial consultation, with no hidden fees. Some treatments may be available on the NHS.

We recommend weekly or fortnightly face-to-face check-ins, especially in the first few months. Appointments are flexible and typically take 15-20 minutes.

Common side effects of Mounjaro and Wegovy include mild nausea, reduced appetite, and occasional digestive changes. Most side effects are temporary and improve within the first few weeks. We'll monitor you closely at our Sheffield clinic and adjust your dose if needed.

Most patients notice reduced appetite within the first week and see measurable weight loss within 4-8 weeks. Significant results typically appear over 3-6 months.

We'll create a maintenance plan to help you sustain your results long-term. This may include a lower medication dose, ongoing check-ins, and lifestyle strategies.

Medical weight loss isn't suitable for everyone. It's not recommended if you're pregnant, breastfeeding, or have certain medical conditions. We'll discuss your medical history during your consultation.
