FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions

Get answers to the questions we hear most often. Can't find what you're looking for? Give us a call.

You may be suitable if you have a BMI of 30 or above, or 27+ with weight-related health conditions such as high blood pressure or type 2 diabetes. We'll assess your eligibility during your free consultation.

Consultation fees and medication costs vary depending on your treatment plan. We'll provide full pricing during your initial consultation, with no hidden fees. Some treatments may be available on the NHS.

We recommend weekly or fortnightly face-to-face check-ins, especially in the first few months. Appointments are flexible and typically take 15-20 minutes.

Common side effects include mild nausea, reduced appetite, and occasional digestive changes. Most side effects are temporary and improve within the first few weeks. We'll monitor you closely and adjust your dose if needed.

Most patients notice reduced appetite within the first week and see measurable weight loss within 4-8 weeks. Significant results typically appear over 3-6 months.

We'll create a maintenance plan to help you sustain your results long-term. This may include a lower medication dose, ongoing check-ins, and lifestyle strategies.

Medical weight loss isn't suitable for everyone. It's not recommended if you're pregnant, breastfeeding, or have certain medical conditions. We'll discuss your medical history during your consultation.
