UNDERSTANDING FLU

Why You Need a Flu Jab

Influenza (flu) is a highly contagious viral infection affecting thousands every winter. It's more serious than a common cold and can lead to complications

Flu Symptoms

High fever (38°C+)
Extreme tiredness
Dry cough
Headache
Body aches
Sore throat
Loss of appetite
Difficulty sleeping

Why Flu Vaccination is Important

Every winter, flu causes thousands of hospitalizations across the UK. Flu vaccinations at Sheffield Wellness Clinic help protect families from:

Pneumonia

Serious lung infection

Bronchitis

Inflammation of airways

Worsening Existing Conditions

Asthma, diabetes, heart disease

Time Off Work or School

Disruption to daily life

Spreading to Vulnerable People

Protecting those around you

ANNUAL VACCINATION

Why You Need a Flu Jab Every Year

Flu Viruses Change Annually

Flu viruses mutate constantly. Each year's flu vaccine is updated to match the strains predicted to circulate. Last year's flu jab won't protect against this year's flu.

Immunity Decreases Over Time

Protection from your flu vaccination gradually decreases. Annual flu jabs at Sheffield Wellness Clinic ensure optimal protection throughout each winter.

Best Time for Flu Jabs

September to early November 2026 is ideal, giving you protection before flu season peaks (December 2026-February 2027). However, it's never too late - get your flu jab anytime during flu season.

Egg Allergies - Flu Jabs

Most flu vaccines contain small amounts of egg protein. However, people with egg allergies can safely receive flu jabs at Sheffield Wellness Clinic:

  • People with egg allergies can safely get flu jabs
  • We safely administer flu vaccines to patients with egg allergies
  • Discuss your allergy during your consultation
  • We'll monitor you after vaccination

Severe egg allergy (anaphylaxis)? We can arrange specialist flu vaccination if needed.

SIDE EFFECTS & SAFETY

Flu Jab Side Effects & Safety

Common Side Effects (Normal & Expected)

At Injection Site:

  • Slight soreness where flu jab was given
  • Redness or swelling
  • Usually resolves within 1-2 days

General:

  • Mild headache after flu vaccination
  • Slight temperature
  • Muscle aches
  • Fatigue
  • Usually resolves within 1-2 days

Important: These mild side effects show your immune system is responding to the flu jab. You cannot get flu from the flu vaccine.

Serious Side Effects

Extremely rare in flu vaccinations. Severe allergic reactions occur in less than 1 in 1 million doses. Sheffield Wellness Clinic has emergency equipment on-site.

Safe & Effective

Flu vaccines have been used safely for decades. Millions of people receive flu jabs every year with excellent safety records.

Expert Monitoring

Our experienced team monitors all patients after vaccination and is trained to handle any rare reactions.

Emergency Equipment

Sheffield Wellness Clinic has full emergency equipment and trained staff on-site for your safety.
